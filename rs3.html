Literature Seminar on Conformal Field Theories

January - April '14

With a little help from Prof. Sujay K. Ashok, this seminar will discuss conformal field theories from the ground up! There is, at the moment, no fixed syllabus or list of topics we'd like to cover. We plan to tailor the topics discussed to reflect our interests over time.

Each meeting will go on for an hour and a half. We meet every Thursday at 9:15 am in Hall 123.
