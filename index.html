about

I study theoretical physics at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences in Chennai.

I am currently working towards a better understanding of \( \mathcal{N} = 2 \) supersymmetric gauge theories. My doctoral adviser is Dr. Sujay K. Ashok.

I'm running a seminar this semester, as usual. We'll be discussing a whole bunch of stuff: the AdS/CFT correspondence, D\( p \)-branes and geometric engineering, supersymmetric gauge theories, and dualities.

I'm going to try my hand at teaching a course this semester, on the topic of classical integrable systems.
