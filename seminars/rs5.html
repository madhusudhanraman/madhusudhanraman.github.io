String Theory Reading Group

January - June '15

The material discussed will roughly revolve around material presented in Polchinski: bosonic strings, conformal field theory, loop amplitudes, D-branes etc. We will highlight other references when we find Polchinski's treatment too terse.

Each meeting will go on for an hour and a half. We meet every Thursday at 6:00 PM in Hall 123.

Note: An asterisk (*) next to a reference means it was mentioned in passing and is only tangentially related to the material discussed in the talk.
